We got you covered with our FAQ's

What can I sell on Tradit?

You can sell different digital products such as online courses, Ebooks, templates, guides, services, coaching sessions, memberships, subscriptions, etc.

What currencies does Tradit support?

Tradit supports payment in 10 currencies. Check our pricing page for details.

How do I get paid?

Payout in naira is credited into your provided bank details a day after transaction, while for other currencies, it varies.

Is Tradit free to use?

Absolutely! Tradit is FREE to use. We also have different subscription packages, so our customers can access more features.

How secure is the platform, especially with my payment details?

We are extremely conscious of securing the payment details of users and our creator’s products from theft. To ensure adequate security, our systems are well protected at the backend via a system we use to secure payment details of users.

How secure are my digital products?

We take security seriously on Tradit. As such, To protect the products and intellectual property of our creators, each user is allocated a unique IP for signing in to take courses. With this, it is reduced to just one device only and this allows for the protection of our creator’s intellectual property.
